### 🎯 What I do

I build production speech AI systems for Arabic and its dialects — fine-tuning **Wav2Vec2**, **Whisper**, and **HiFi-GAN** for ASR, TTS, and full speech-to-speech pipelines. Currently job-hunting for roles where that specialization is the core of the work.

### 💬 Ask me about

- Arabic ASR, TTS, and speech-to-speech pipelines
- Fine-tuning Wav2Vec2, Whisper, HiFi-GAN
- Pronunciation & fluency assessment (forced alignment, CTC)
- Deep Learning (PyTorch, TensorFlow, Hugging Face Transformers)
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Math for ML (Linear Algebra, Calculus, Probability & Statistics)
